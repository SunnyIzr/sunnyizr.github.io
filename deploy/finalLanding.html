Questions
What do I need to do?
You’ll download a test version of the app and check in with your coach once a week via the app to work together on your plan.
What happens after three weeks?
After week three, we may reach out to you for feedback, but other than that you’re done and we will disconnect your account. Alternatively, you may continue your work with your Money Mentor at their decision.
Why is this free?
We are testing out a new product and want to learn.
What is a personal finance coach, exactly?
Clasp Money Mentors are personal finance coaches. A personal finance coach is an accountability partner who motivates you and keeps you on task and on track and suggests strategies. They are not fiduciary-bound, and cannot move or manage money, like a financial advisor or money manager.
